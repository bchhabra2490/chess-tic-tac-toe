Chess Tic‑Tac‑Toe
Rules
  • Each side has one pawn, rook, knight, and bishop.
  • On your turn, either place a piece from your side or move one of your pieces.
  • Moving is only allowed after both players have three pieces on the board.
  • Pieces move like in chess on this 4×4 board. Pawns do not promote; when they reach the far rank, they reverse direction on future moves.
  • Captured pieces return to their owner’s side to be placed again.
  • First to get four of their pieces in a row (row, column, or diagonal) wins.
